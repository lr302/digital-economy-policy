## 摘要

This APEC Policy Support Unit report provides the most comprehensive empirical mapping of product-specific rules of origin (PSRs) across nine selected trade agreements in the APEC region. Mapping all PSRs at the HS 6-digit sub-heading level, the report constructs a novel ROO heterogeneity index and combines econometric analysis with firm-level survey data to demonstrate that divergence across ROO standards significantly undermines preference utilization rates, particularly for MSMEs. The econometric evidence shows a robust negative correlation between ROO heterogeneity and preference utilization. The survey evidence reveals "compliance fatigue" — over 60% of surveyed MSMEs report that managing multiple PTA-specific ROO requirements is their primary barrier to utilizing trade preferences. While focused on goods trade, the report's central insight — that ROO heterogeneity is a fundamental barrier to trade liberalization — carries profound implications for the design of future digital trade origin rules.

## 核心发现

1. 首次在HS 6位编码级别系统映射9个PTA的产品特定原产地规则
2. 构建ROO异质性指数——异质性与优惠利用率显著负相关
3. 超过60%中小微企业面临"合规疲劳"——规则复杂性超过关税优惠吸引力
4. 警示：若不早期协调统一数字贸易原产地标准，将重现"ROO意大利面碗"
